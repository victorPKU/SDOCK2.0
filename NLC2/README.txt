In each directory:

_note: summary of this docking case, including binding coordination number
       chains, size, unnatural amino acid, small molecules
_complex.pdb: whole complex structure
_interface.pdb: complex structure with unique interface
interface_: interface residue list
_l_.pdb: free ligand protein structure
_r_.pdb: free receptor protein structure
_cofactor.pdb: unnatural amino acid or small molecules structure
_l_pair: sequence alignment of ligand
_r_pair: sequence alignment of receptor
